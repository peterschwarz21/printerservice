# Privacy Policy — [Registered Sole Prop Name] Family Notifications SMS Program

**Effective date:** July, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the [Registered Sole Prop Name] Family Notifications SMS messaging program (the "Program"), a private, non-commercial personal notification and family messaging service operated solely by [Registered Sole Prop Name].

## Information We Collect

We collect and store only the following information from Program participants:

- The mobile phone number used to opt in to the Program
- Opt-in and opt-out status and timestamps
- The content of messages sent to and from the Program number

## How We Use Information

Information collected through the Program is used solely to deliver personal notification, reminder, and conversational messages to enrolled members of the Program. We do not use participant information for marketing, advertising, or promotional purposes of any kind.

## SMS Consent and Mobile Information Sharing

**No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.** Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with, sold to, or rented to any third parties, affiliates, or lead generators. Information sharing is limited exclusively to messaging platform service providers (e.g., our SMS delivery provider) acting solely on our behalf to deliver Program messages, and only to the extent necessary to provide that service.

## Opting Out

You may opt out of the Program at any time by replying **STOP** to any Program message. You will receive one final confirmation message and no further messages will be sent. Reply **HELP** to any message for assistance.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Opt-in records and phone numbers are retained while you remain enrolled in the Program and as required for messaging compliance recordkeeping. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting [contact email].

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date above reflects the most recent revision. Continued participation in the Program after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.




# Terms and Conditions — [Registered Sole Prop Name] Family Notifications SMS Program

**Effective date:** July, 2026

These Terms and Conditions govern participation in the Schwarz Family Notifications SMS messaging program (the "Program").

## 1. Program Description

The Program is a private, non-commercial personal notification and family messaging service operated solely by [Registered Sole Prop Name]. The Program sends automated personal alerts and reminders (for example, home automation notifications and household or schedule reminders) and supports two-way conversational messaging among a closed group consisting of the account owner and invited family members.

The Program sends no marketing or promotional content, contains no third-party content, and does not send messages on behalf of any other person or business. Enrollment is by personal invitation only and is not available to the general public.

## 2. Opting In

Invited family members enroll either by texting the keyword **JOIN** from their own mobile phone to the Program number, or by giving verbal consent directly to the Program operator as described on our [SMS Verbal Opt-In](./optin) page. By opting in, you consent to receive recurring automated personal notification and conversational messages from [Registered Sole Prop Name] at the mobile number used. Consent is not a condition of any purchase. Upon enrollment you will receive a confirmation message identifying the Program, message frequency, and opt-out and help instructions.

## 3. Message Frequency and Rates

Message frequency varies based on triggered notifications and member conversation. **Message and data rates may apply** according to your mobile carrier plan. Contact your carrier for details.

## 4. Opting Out

Reply **STOP** to any Program message to cancel at any time. You will receive a single confirmation message stating that you have been unsubscribed, and no further messages will be sent to your number.

## 5. Help and Support

Reply **HELP** to any Program message for assistance, or contact [contact email].

## 6. Supported Keywords

- **JOIN** — opt in to the Program
- **STOP** — opt out of the Program
- **HELP** — request assistance

## 7. Carrier Disclaimer

Mobile carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

## 8. Eligibility and Termination

Participation is limited to the account owner and invited family members. The Program operator may modify, suspend, or discontinue the Program, or revoke any participant's enrollment, at any time.

## 9. Privacy

Participant information is handled as described in our [Privacy Policy](./) above. No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
