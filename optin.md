# SMS Verbal Opt-In — [Registered Sole Prop Name] Family Notifications SMS Program

**Effective date:** July, 2026

This page describes how verbal consent is collected for the [Registered Sole
Prop Name] Family Notifications SMS Program (the "Program"), a private,
non-commercial personal notification and family messaging service. Enrollment
is by personal invitation only and is not available to the general public.

## How Verbal Consent Is Collected

Invited family members may opt in to the Program by giving verbal consent
directly to the Program operator, in person or over the phone. Before consent
is accepted, the Program operator reads the following disclosure to the
participant:

> "You are agreeing to receive automated personal notification, reminder, and
> conversational text messages from [Registered Sole Prop Name] at the mobile
> number you provide. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may
> apply. You can reply STOP at any time to cancel, or reply HELP for help.
> Consent is not a condition of any purchase. Do you agree to receive these
> messages at this number?"

Consent is accepted only when the participant clearly answers yes. The Program
operator records the participant's name, phone number, and the date of
consent. Upon enrollment, the participant receives a confirmation text message
identifying the Program and repeating the message frequency, opt-out, and help
information.

## What You Are Opting In To

- Automated personal alerts and reminders (for example, home automation
  notifications and household or schedule reminders)
- Two-way conversational messages within a closed family group

The Program sends no marketing or promotional content. **No mobile information
will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional
purposes.**

## Opting Out and Help

- Reply **STOP** to any message to cancel at any time. You will receive one
  final confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.
- Reply **HELP** to any message for assistance, or contact [contact email].

**Message and data rates may apply.** Message frequency varies.

See our [Privacy Policy and Terms &amp; Conditions](./) for full details on how
participant information is handled.
